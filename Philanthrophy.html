Scholarship Fund

He established the Virgil Abloh "Post-Modern" Scholarship Fund and raised $1 million to assist Black students. Virgil used his platform to offer the Black community an opportunity to chase their dreams and defy the status quo.

Fundraising Program

In 2020, Abloh's brand Off-White launched a fundraising program called "I Support Young Black Businesses" that sold Hoodies and T-Shirts with “I Support Young Black Businesses” written on them. All of the money raised went to an organization called Chicago CRED, aimed to diminish gun violence.

Virgil Abloh and Off-White™ collaborated with the leading
academic medical center, Cleveland Clinic, in 2020. They created
a reusable mask, medically tested and sustainably produced.
All the money from the sales of this mask will be directed
to support COVID-19-related research at the Cleveland
Clinic’s Center for Global and Emerging Pathogens. Off-White™
also made a monetary donation to the center and created a
limited edition t-shirt that reads “I Support Our HealthCare Heroes”.
